FAQ

Questions we hear most often.

How much do your services cost?
Every engagement is scoped to your goals, so pricing depends on what you need. A content-only package costs less than full content, social, and paid management. After your free audit, you receive a proposal with fixed monthly pricing before anything begins. No surprises, no hidden fees.
Do I have to sign a long-term contract?
No. We work month to month. We would rather earn your business with results every thirty days than lock you into a twelve-month agreement.
How quickly will I see results?
It depends on the channel. Paid campaigns generate data and leads within the first few weeks. Organic social growth typically builds meaningfully over three to six months of consistent publishing. Either way, you receive a monthly report showing exactly where things stand.
Do you create the content, or do we have to?
We create it. That includes planning, on-location filming with professional camera and drone equipment, editing, and captions. Your involvement is a content day every so often and final approval. We handle everything in between.
Which platforms do you work with?
Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and LinkedIn for content and social, plus Google Ads and Meta Ads for paid campaigns. During your audit we recommend the two or three platforms where your customers actually are, rather than spreading your budget across all of them.
Do you only work with businesses in New Jersey?
We're based in Mahwah and film on location across North Jersey and the New York metro area. For strategy, social management, and paid advertising, we work with clients anywhere.
What do you need from us to get started?
Very little: access to your social accounts and ad accounts, your brand assets if you have them, and a kickoff conversation about your goals. We take it from there.
How do you report on performance?
You receive a monthly report covering reach, followers, leads, and cost per lead, with a short summary of what we're doing next and why. We report on the numbers that affect your revenue, not vanity metrics.
